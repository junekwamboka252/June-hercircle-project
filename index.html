"A safe and inclusive space for women."

HerCircle was born from a simple but powerful realization that women crave supportive spaces where they can share their experiences without judgment and also connect. Founded by June Kwamboka, who has a passion for supporting women, HerCircle aims to make it happen.

What we do: We provide a nurturing online community where women can share, learn, and grow together through mentorships and workshops.

OUR VISION & MISSION

Our Mission: To empower women to build strong, supportive communities that celebrate every voice and story, so no woman ever feels alone on her journey.

Our Vision: We envision a world where every woman has a safe, uplifting space to connect, grow, and thrive — a sisterhood that transcends borders and empowers women to lead with confidence and compassion.

OUR GOALS

  • 1. Two virtual events to inspire and connect women.
  • 2. Reach 1,000 active users in our online community.
  • 3. Launch a Mentorship Program to connect experienced women with those just starting.
